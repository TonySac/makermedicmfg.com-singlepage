About Maker Medic Manufacturing

After several years of hobby 3D printing and design, Maker Medic Manufacturing was founded in 2024. Owned and operated by a full time flight paramedic with close to 10 years in emergency medical services, ranging from 911 ground transport to critical care and flight medicine, and a background in mechanical engineering.

Anyone in the field, including myself, has experienced the frustrations of misplacing a syringe, breaking a medication vial, or not being able to find what you need when you need it. The aim of Maker Medic Manufacturing is to create things that add a little flair and make working in the EMS or healthcare setting a little easier.

As we've grown, our offerings have expanded to include decorative items, print on demand services, and custom designs not limited to EMS or healthcare.
